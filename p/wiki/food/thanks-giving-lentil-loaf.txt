- 1 medium onion
- 1 large carrot
- 1 large pepper
- 2 stalks of celery
- 3 cloves of garlic
- 3/4 c. of lentils
- 3/4 c. of bulgar wheat
- 3 1/3 cups of water
- 1 table spoon of cumin
- 1 table spoon of tumeric
- 1 teaspoon of chili powder (or more; I like things spicy)
- 1 teaspoon of cinnamon
- salt to taste

# dice onions, carrots, & celery.
# sautee the onion
# just before it's carmalized, add the carrots & celery.
# sautee for 5 to 10 minutes, stirring constantly
# add the water & lentils
# cook until lentils & veggies are soft
# puree the veggie & lentil mix until chunky-smooth
# add the Bulgar Wheat, stirring constantly as the Bulgar starts to soften
# Add herbs, spices, and salt
# place in a meatloaf pan with parchment paper
# top the loaf with juliened peppers.
