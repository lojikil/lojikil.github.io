  • 1 medium onion
  • 1 large carrot
  • 1 large pepper
  • 2 stalks of celery
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 3/4 c. of lentils
  • 3/4 c. of bulgar wheat
  • 3 1/3 cups of water
  • 1 table spoon of cumin
  • 1 table spoon of tumeric
  • 1 teaspoon of chili powder (or more; I like things spicy)
  • 1 teaspoon of cinnamon
  • salt to taste
  1. dice onions, carrots, & celery.
  2. sautee the onion
  3. just before it's carmalized, add the carrots & celery.
  4. sautee for 5 to 10 minutes, stirring constantly
  5. add the water & lentils
  6. cook until lentils & veggies are soft
  7. puree the veggie & lentil mix until chunky-smooth
  8. add the Bulgar Wheat, stirring constantly as the Bulgar starts to soften
  9. Add herbs, spices, and salt
  10. place in a meatloaf pan with parchment paper
  11. top the loaf with juliened peppers.
I was originally following this lentil loaf recipe, but parted my separate ways when I realized I didn't have most of that stuff (nor can I eat Tamari or the like).
